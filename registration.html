BGIS 2026
How to Register

India's biggest open-for-all BGMI tournament. Form your squad of 4, pick your qualifier slot, and compete for ₹2 Crore+. Open to all players — no invite needed.

1

Select Your Tournament

Choose BGIS 2026 or another active tournament from the dropdown.

2

Enter Captain Details

Provide your BGMI UID, in-game name, email and phone number.

3

Add Teammates

Enter the BGMI UIDs of your 3 teammates. All must have active BGMI accounts.

4

Choose Match Slot

Pick an available qualifier slot. Limited slots per region — book early!
